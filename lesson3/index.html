Site Name

This site is going to be called MainLandvalley.com. Just as the name indicates, it would a website for the MainLand valley community and its businesses hence the name MainLandvalley.com.

Domain name: MainLandvalley.com

Site Purpose

The MainLand valley chamber website is dedicated to creating a community of businesses in the MainLand valley community. Each business represents a member of the MainLand chamber of commerce. This organization is committed to promoting the business interests of the members of the organization and promoting the area, attracting additional businesses, tourism and people. Also, by clicking on the MainLand valley website, people can have an overview of the MainLand community, it's people, weather and businesses located in the MainLand valley community.
